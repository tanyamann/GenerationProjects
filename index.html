Lions are the strongest kids on the block

Lions aren't called the king of the jungle for no reason. Not counting humans, they are at the top of the food chain. The majority of lions live in Africa.

The cats that like to link up

Lions live in groups called prides. They work together and play together. The females do most of the hunting and the males protect the pride.

The ladies really run this pack

The females do the majority of hunting while also looking after the pride's cubs. The females work together for a smooth running pride.

Don't become this cat's snack

With their massive strength and speed they are excellent hunters. If it moves, they eat it!

Lion traits Some facts
Strongest on the block They run at speeds of 50 miles/hr. Can lift 2,300 pounds
Cats like to link up Lions are the only true social big cat. They live in prides.
Ladies run the pack The main role of the female lion is to hunt. They also take care of the cubs, feeding and protecting them
Cat's snack They're carnivores. They eat birds, rabbits, turtles, lizards, wild dogs, antelopes, cheetah, buffaloes, crocodiles, giraffes.
