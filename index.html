A look at Lion traits Some fun facts
Strongest on the block There's a reason that lions are called the king of the jungle. Not counting humans, they are at the top of the food chain. The majority of lions live in Africa. With their massive strength and speed they are excellent hunters. They run at speeds of 50 miles/hr. Can lift 2,300 pounds
Cats like to link up Lions live in groups called prides. They work together and play together. The females do most of the hunting and the males protect the pride. Lions are the only true social big cat.
Ladies run the pack The females work together for a smooth running pride. The main role of the female lion is to hunt. They also take care of the cubs,feed and protect them
Cat's snack With their massive strength and speed they are excellent hunters. They're carnivores. They eat birds, rabbits, turtles, lizards, wild dogs, antelopes, cheetah, buffaloes, crocodiles, giraffes. If it moves, they eat it!
